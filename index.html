PUTTING FEARS TO REST

If the thought of sitting in a dental chair makes you anxious and afraid, you are not alone. In fact, thousands of people put off seeing the dentist every year and risk the health of their smile and teeth because of their fears. Dental visits are second in phobias only to public speaking. Not only does Dr. Schneider understand this he has been studying methods for dealing with these fears since his undergraduate days. He will do everything possible to make your dental experience pleasant, maybe even fun.

Indulge Yourself in a Spa-Like Setting
